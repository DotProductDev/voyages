Web Resources

The websites listed here provide a variety of resources, including but not limited to lesson plans, interactive maps, and narratives of enslaved Africans.

The Virginia Foundation for the Humanities, the Digital Media Lab at the University of Virginia Library

Schomburg Center, New York Public Library

WWW address http://www.inmotionaame.org

Slave Trade and broader experiences of Slavery in North America

Breaking the Silence

Abolition of the Slave Trade
BBC

Les Anneaux de la Mémoire: Alliance Internationale
